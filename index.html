THE WESTLIFE BAND

Some people feed you with love.

Westlife is a pop boy band from Ireland and was formed in 1998, the group's boss is Louis Walsh. The group has enjoyed great success in the UK and Ireland as well as in other countries in Europe and parts of Australia, Asia and Africa.

Westlife has had 14 UK #1 singles (from 1999 to 2006), ranking 3rd among the artists and bands with the most #1 singles in the UK (behind Elvis Presley and The Beatles, tied with Cliff Richard). Westlife are the only band in UK chart history to have seven consecutive #1 singles and win UK Record of the Year four times. The band has sold a total of more than 44 million albums in over 40 countries including more than 12 million albums in the UK alone, 14 times a #1 single in the UK and 13 times a #1 single in Ireland.

On October 19, 2011, the four members of Westlife suddenly announced their disbandment and announced that they would be having a farewell tour in the spring of 2012.[1] By 2018, the group had reunited and released their 11th studio album, Spectrum, in 2019.
